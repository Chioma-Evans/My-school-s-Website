Our Classes

Daycare

Focuses on care, bonding, sensory exploration, and early physical development also, safe and nurturing care for children.

Crèche

Focuses on early communication, social interaction, basic independence, and play-based learning also, fun activities that encourage early growth.

Pre-Nursery

Focuses on language development, basic counting, routines, and early learning habits also, introduction to sounds, numbers and play.

Nursery 1

Focuses on early literacy, number, recognition, writing readiness, and cooperative play also, building confidence through learning.

Nursery 2

Focuses on reading simple sentences, basic arithmetic, problem-solving, and teamwork skills also, developing social and communication skills.

Nursery 3

Focuses on fluent reading, sentence writing, basic math skills, and primary school readiness also, preparing children for primary education.
